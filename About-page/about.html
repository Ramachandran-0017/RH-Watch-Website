Our Story

Built by collectors, for collectors.

RH Multi Watch was founded on a simple frustration: it was nearly impossible to find a dealer who treated a watch as more than inventory. So we built one — a house where every timepiece is authenticated, every mechanism understood, and every customer treated like the collector they are, whether it's their first watch or their fiftieth.

Today we carry pieces from more than forty maisons, from entry-level daily-wearers to museum-grade complications, all backed by the same authenticity guarantee we made on day one.

What We Stand For

The Principles Behind Every Sale

Authenticity, Verified

Every watch is inspected by certified horologists before it ever reaches a shelf — no exceptions, no shortcuts.

Craftsmanship First

We work directly with in-house watchmakers for servicing and restoration, preserving movements the way they were built.

Honest Guidance

No commission-driven upselling. Our specialists help you find the right watch, not just the most expensive one.

Relationships Over Sales

Many of our clients have shopped with us for over a decade. We're building a house you'll return to.

Our Journey
